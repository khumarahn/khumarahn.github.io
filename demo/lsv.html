Iterates of the transfer operator

Let \(L\) be the transfer operator with respect to the Lebesgue measure: \[ (L v)(x) = \sum_{y \in T^{-1} x} \frac{v(y)}{T'(y)} \] We are interested in the iterates \(L^n v\) for functions \(v\) with \(\|v - 1\|_{C^1}\) small. We would like to prove that \(L^n v\) is monotone on \([1/2,1]\) in the sense that \[ (L^{n+1}v)(x) \leq (L^n v)(x) \quad \text{for all } n \geq 0 \text{ and } x \in [1/2,1] \] But how?

Density and derivatives

Here we plot \(L^n 1\) with its first two derivatives as an approximation of the invariant density.

Three conditions

Let \(h\) denote the density of the invariant measure and let \(w = 1 / T'\). For \( x \in [0,1] \) let \( y_1 < y_2 \) denote the two preimages of \(x\) under \(T\). Suppose that for each \(x\): \[ \Bigl[ \frac{h'}{h} w + w' \Bigr](y_1) \leq \Bigl[ \frac{h'}{h} w + w' \Bigr](y_2) \tag{C1} \] and \[ \Bigl[ \frac{h''}{h} w^2 + 3 \frac{h'}{h} w' w + w'' w + (w')^2 \Bigr](y_1) \geq \Bigl[ \frac{h''}{h} w^2 + 3 \frac{h'}{h} w' w + w'' w + (w')^2 \Bigr](y_2) . \tag{C2} \] Suppose also that for \(x\) on the second branch, \[ x \geq \frac{y_1}{1 + \frac{h(y_2) w(y_2)}{h(y_1) w(y_1)}} + \frac{y_2}{1 + \frac{h(y_1) w(y_1)}{h(y_2) w(y_2)}} . \tag{C3} \]
If the three conditions above are satisfied, then there is an argument (which has to be checked!) that the cone of positive functions satisfying \(\frac{v'}{v} \geq \frac{h'}{h}\) and \(\frac{v''}{v} \leq \frac{h''}{h}\) is invariant under iterations of \(L\), and that \( (L v)(x) \leq v(x) \) for all such \(v\) and \(x \in [1/2,1]\).
It is not clear however how to verify these conditions. But we can compute an approximation for \(h\) and plot some graphs. Below C1, C2 and C3 are the the differences of the two sides in corresponding conditions, taken so that positive values mean that the condition holds.
